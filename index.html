This endpoint is for querying wikipedia for city, state

Format:

City,_State

Examples:

curl https://query-wikipedia.herokuapp.com/\?params\=San_Francisco,_California

Response:

Martinez is a city and the county seat of Contra Costa County, California, in the East Bay of the San Francisco Bay Area. The population was 35,824 at the 2010 census. The downtown is notable for its large number of preserved old buildings and antique shops. Martinez is located on the south side of the Carquinez Strait in the San Francisco Bay Area, directly facing the cities of Benicia and Vallejo.
